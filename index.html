Visit Portland

Enjoy the best the Pacific Northwest has to offer in Portland and the surrounding areas.

Top three activities to do in Portland

Saturday Market

Spend afternoon downtown at Portland's Saturday Market. Art, food, and lovely waterfront views.

Council Crest Park

Have a picnic with beautiful views of the city and Cascade Mountains beyond.

Sauvie Island

Enjoy a bike ride around the idyllic Sauvie Island. Pick fresh produce yourself and eat it on Sauvie's clothing optional beach.
